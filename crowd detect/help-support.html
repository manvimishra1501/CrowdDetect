Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a live video feed?
Go to Live Feed page → Click "Choose Video" → Select your video or CCTV feed → Click "Generate Heatmap".
How do I contact the nearest authority?
Go to Contact Authority page → Select issue type → Share location → Click "Send Alert".
How is the crowd density calculated?
Crowd density is based on video feed analysis and heatmap mapping with estimated crowd points on the map.

Tips for Crowd Monitoring

  • Always check live feed for sudden spikes in crowd density.
  • Enable notifications for alerts in high-risk areas.
  • Communicate regularly with authorities using the "Send Message" feature.
  • Regularly analyze social trends to predict upcoming crowd gatherings.
  • Ensure GPS location sharing is enabled for accurate alerts.
